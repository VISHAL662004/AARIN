Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out our contact form, calling our office directly, or sending us an email. One of our research consultants will contact you within 24 hours to set up a time that works for you.

What information should I provide when contacting you?

When contacting us, it's helpful to provide details about your research area, current stage of your academic journey (e.g., starting PhD, working on thesis, etc.), the specific services you're interested in, and any deadlines you're working with.

Do you offer remote consultations?

Yes, we offer remote consultations via Zoom, Skype, or phone for clients who cannot visit our office in person. Many of our clients work with us entirely online, from anywhere in the world.

What are your service fees?

Our service fees vary depending on the specific requirements of your project, including the scope of work, timeline, and complexity. We offer competitive rates and customizable packages to meet different budgets. Contact us for a personalized quote.

How do you ensure confidentiality?

We take confidentiality very seriously. All client information and research materials are treated with the utmost confidentiality. We sign non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) when requested and have strict data protection policies in place.
